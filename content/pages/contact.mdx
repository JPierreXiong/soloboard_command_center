## Contact Us

Have a question about your digital vault, feedback on our inheritance logic, or a feature request? We'd love to hear from you! Our team is dedicated to ensuring your digital legacy is secured and your loved ones are protected.

### Get in Touch

- **Email Support**: support@digitalheirloom.app
- **Response Time**: We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.
- **Technical Support**: If you are reporting an issue with vault access or heartbeat check-in, please include your account email (but **NEVER** share your Master Password) for faster troubleshooting.
- **Beneficiary Inquiries**: If you are a beneficiary trying to initiate the inheritance process, please refer to the instructions provided in the notification email you received.

### Security Warning

Afterglow staff will **NEVER** ask for your Master Password or your private Fragment A/B codes. Please stay vigilant against phishing attempts.
