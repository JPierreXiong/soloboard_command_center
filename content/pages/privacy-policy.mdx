**Effective Date: February 11, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

SoloBoard ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our unified monitoring dashboard platform.

## 2. Data We Collect

We collect only the information essential for providing our service:

**Identity & Contact Data**: Email address, name. Purpose: Account creation, authentication, and service notifications.

**Financial Data**: Payment history and subscription tier (Free/Base/Pro). We do not store full credit card numbers; payments are processed via secure third parties (Stripe, Creem).

**Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, and login logs. Purpose: Security monitoring and fraud prevention.

**API Credentials**: Your third-party API keys (Google Analytics, Stripe, etc.). Important: These credentials are encrypted using AES-256-CBC before storage. We use them only to fetch data on your behalf and never share them with third parties.

**Usage Data**: Dashboard views, site configurations, and sync logs. Purpose: To provide and improve our service.

## 3. Data Security

Your API keys and sensitive data are protected through:

**Encryption**: All API keys are encrypted using AES-256-CBC before storage.

**Secure Transmission**: All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

**Access Control**: Only authorized systems can access encrypted credentials, and only for the purpose of syncing your data.

**No Data Sharing**: We never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

**Contractual Necessity**: To provide dashboard monitoring and data synchronization services.

**Legal Obligation**: For tax records or regulatory compliance.

**Legitimate Interests**: To maintain the security and integrity of our platform.

## 5. Cookies and Third-Party Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

**Essential Cookies**: For maintaining your session and authentication.

**Analytics Cookies**: We use analytics to understand how users interact with our platform. You can opt-out via your browser settings.

## 6. Data Retention

**Active Accounts**: Data is kept as long as your account is active.

**Inactive Accounts**: If an account remains inactive for over 12 months, we may delete associated data following our data retention policies.

**API Keys**: Deleted immediately upon account deletion or when you remove a data source.

## 7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
- Request data portability
- Opt-out of marketing communications

## 8. Third-Party Services

SoloBoard integrates with third-party services (Google Analytics, Stripe, Shopify, etc.). When you connect these services, you authorize us to access data according to their respective privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."

## 10. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries, contact us at: **support@soloboard.app**
